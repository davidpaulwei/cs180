  1. Project 1 : Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii Photo Collection
     Prokudin-Gorskii photographed the Russian Empire using black-and-white negatives with red, green and blue filters, hoping future technologies could stack the three layers to produce colored image. I take the digitized negatives of Prokudin-Gorskii's work to produce the RGB color image. The original three color layers from Prokudin-Gorskii's work are not accurately aligned, so I designed an alignment algorithm to preprocess the layers before stacking them together.
